Summary

This mod adds "Spookyfest" and "Flower Day" holidays to the calendar. You can pick and choose, so if you only want one, just install one.

Important: This mod requires my Custom Holiday Utility mod. You must install it as well, or Extra Holidays will not work.

If you report that Extra Holidays isn't working and I find out you do not have Custom Holiday Utility installed, I will kick you from my server.

Spookyfest

Spookyfest occurs during Fall, and does not come with a day off of work and school.

Schedule

  • 7 day seasons → Monday of week 1
  • 14 day seasons → Friday of week 1
  • 28 day seasons → Friday of week 3

Flower Day

Flower Day occurs during Spring, and is observed by a day off of work and school on Mondays.

Schedule

  • 7 day seasons → Monday of week 1
  • 14 day seasons → Monday of week 1
  • 28 day seasons → Monday of week 2

Though it is based on Easter, Monday was chosen instead of Sunday because (1) Sunday holidays do not work when you start in that season, (2) being on Sunday would put Flower Day on New Year's Day, and (3) this way you can have the day after off of work and school.
