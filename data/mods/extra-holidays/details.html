Summary

This mod adds three new holidays: "Spookyfest", "Flower Day", and "Simdependence Day". They are each in their own package, so only install the one(s) you want.

Important Notices

This mod requires my Custom Holiday Utility mod. You must install it as well, or none of these holidays will work.

If you want these holidays to appear in an existing save, you must run the cheat chu.add_missing_holidays.

Spookyfest

Occurs during Fall. Does NOT come with a day off from work and school.

Schedule

  • 7 day seasons → Monday of week 1
  • 14 day seasons → Friday of week 1
  • 28 day seasons → Friday of week 3

Flower Day

Occurs during Spring. Comes with a day off from work and school.

Schedule

  • 7 day seasons → Monday of week 1
  • 14 day seasons → Monday of week 1
  • 28 day seasons → Monday of week 2

Though it is based on Easter, Monday was chosen instead of Sunday because (1) Sunday holidays do not work when you start in that season, (2) being on Sunday would put Flower Day on New Year's Day, and (3) this way you can have the day after off of work and school.

Simdependence Day

Occurs during Summer. Comes with a day off from work and school.

Schedule

  • 7 day seasons → Wednesday of week 1
  • 14 day seasons → Wednesday of week 1
  • 28 day seasons → Wednesday of week 2
