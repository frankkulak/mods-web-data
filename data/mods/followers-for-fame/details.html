Automatic followers

  • Gain followers when you gain fame.

    • Interactions that add followers: Selling a painting or photo, making an app or game, licensing a song or lyrics, publishing a book, being nominated for an award, accepting an award, completing a work day as an actor, ranking up as a celebrity, and getting positive PR through the PR agency.

  • Lose followers when you lose fame.

    • Interactions that remove followers: Ranking down as a celebrity and getting bad PR through the PR agency.


Exact amounts for gains/losses are random, but are influenced by the sim's interaction, current fame rank, and current follower count.

Example: A non-famous sim with 10 followers will gain very few for a painting, but a famous sim with 10 million followers will gain hundreds of thousands for a movie.

Initial followers for famous sims

When you first load a save with this mod installed, all of your famous sims will have their follower counts checked. If they do not have any followers, then they will be given a random amount that corresponds with their fame level.

1 star = ~1k, 2 stars = ~10k, 3 stars = ~100k, 4 stars = ~1M, 5 stars = ~10M

Customization & cheats

For all of the following, you will need to enable testing cheats with the testingcheats on command.


Disabling automatic followers for a sim

All sims have auto followers on by default, but you can turn it off for specific sims by shift-clicking them and selecting the "Follower Cheats" menu option.


Manually add/remove followers

You can add/remove followers with interactions by shift-clicking a sim and going to the "Follower Cheats" menu option.

Alternatively, you can also use the following console cheats. They each accept 3 arguments, the first being the number of followers, the second being the sim's first name, and the third being their last name.

  • fkff.add_followers → Add the specified number of followers to the named sim.

    • Example: fkff.add_followers 500 bella goth → Add 500 followers to Bella Goth

  • fkff.remove_followers → Remove the specified number of followers from the named sim.

    • Example: fkff.remove_followers 1000 eliza pancakes → Remove 1,000 followers from Eliza Pancakes

  • fkff.set_followers → Set the named sim's followers to the specified amount.

    • Example: fkff.set_followers 50000000 judith ward → Set Judith Ward's follower count to 50 million.
