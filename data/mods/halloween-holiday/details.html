Summary

This mod adds a holiday called "Halloween" to the calendar by default. That's it, ta-da ðŸŽ‰.

If you want the holiday to be called something else, such as "Spookyfest", change the contents of the HolidayNameOverride.txt file.

Schedule

Halloween is scheduled during Fall, always before Harvestfest. The exact day depends on your Season length:

  • 7 days → Monday of week 1
  • 14 days → Friday of week 1
  • 28 days → Friday of week 3
