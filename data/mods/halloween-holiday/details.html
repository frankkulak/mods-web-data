Schedule

Halloween is scheduled during Fall. The exact day depends on your Season length:

  • 7 days → Monday of week 1
  • 14 days → Friday of week 1
  • 28 days → Friday of week 3

This schedule ensures that Halloween occurs before Harvestfest, which is always the last Thursday of Fall.
