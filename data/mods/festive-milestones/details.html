Summary

This mod adds milestones for all events, festivals, and holidays in City Living, Island Living, Seasons, and Snowy Escape.

City Living

There are 5 milestones for the festivals and events that occur in San Myshuno. To get these, you need to be nearby the area while they are happening.

  • GeekCon
  • Humor & Hijinks Festival
  • Romance Festival
  • Spice Festival
  • Flea Market

Island Living

There are 8 milestones for events and festivals that occur in Sulani. To get them, you must actively participate, such as eating food at the Town Potluck or cheering on the baby turtles at the Turtle Hatching.

  • Family Fun Day
  • Flowers & Music Festival
  • Town BBQ
  • Town Potluck
  • Island Celebration
  • Beach Bonfire
  • Fishing Competition
  • Turtle Hatching

Seasons

There are 8 milestones for holidays and meeting special sims from Seasons. To get the milestone for a holiday, you must complete enough traditions to get at least a silver medal for it. To get the milestones for meeting special sims, you have to talk to them.

  • Flower Day*
  • Harvestfest
  • Love Day
  • New Year's Eve
  • Spookyfest*
  • Winterfest
  • Meeting Father Winter
  • Meeting Flower Bunny

* There are no official "Spookyfest" or "Flower Day" holidays in Seasons; these come from my Extra Holidays mod, which must also be installed in order for these milestones to work.

Snowy Escape

There are 4 milestones for festivals and meeting special sims in Mt. Komorebi. To get the milestones for festivals, you just need to be in the area while the festival is happening. To get the milestone for meeting Yamachan, you have to... meet Yamachan.

  • Festival of Light
  • Festival of Snow
  • Festival of Youth
  • Meeting Yamachan

Cheats

To add all milestones associated with a pack to the active sim, run fkfm.add_all <pack_code>, where the pack code is one of ep03 (City Living), ep05 (Seasons), ep07 (Island Living), or ep10 (Snowy Escape).
