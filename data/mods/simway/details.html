This mod is an idea I had a while back, and has been a "work in progress" for years. I have been focused on other things, such as Sims 4 Toolkit, and have therefore not been making progress on it. There is no estimated release date at this time, but I do still plan on finishing it. Apologies for the massive delay, I know people have been looking forward to it.

Planned features

  • At least three different MLMs for your sims to be a part of.
  • You may be approached by an MLM member who wants to sell products to you, recruit you, or both.
  • You have to pay a startup fee to join an MLM and a portion of your profits to remain in it.
  • You can earn money by selling products or recruiting other sims.
  • Host and attend "selling parties" for MLM products.
  • Various MLMs may pop up every now and then to wreak havoc on your sims.
