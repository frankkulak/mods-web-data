"Simway" has been a work-in-progress for years, and has been paused while I focus on other things, such as Sims 4 Toolkit. I am pleased to announce it it now back in full-swing development, and is my primary focus. There is no estimated release date at this time.

Planned features

  • At least three different MLMs for your sims to be a part of.
  • You may be approached by MLM members who want to sell products or recruit you.
  • You can earn money by selling products or recruiting other sims.
  • You have to pay a startup fee to join an MLM and a portion of your profits to remain in it. Want out? Have fun with that :)
  • Host and attend "selling parties" for custom MLM products.
