What does this mod do?

It silences "Discovery Quest" phone calls and notifications. That's it.

Isn't this in my game settings?

Yes, but it does not prevent popups that have already been scheduled.

Your existing save(s) likely already have quite a few of these.

This mod has two advantages over the setting:

  • It silences Discovery Quests by default, in every save
  • It works on Discovery Quests that have already been scheduled

Known issues

  • This mod also silences Reaper's Rewards popups, as they are implemented as Discovery Quests.
