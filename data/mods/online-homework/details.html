How to use

Click on any computer (including most CC) to do homework.

  • Children/Teens: Select the "Do Homework" interaction.
  • University Students: Go to the "University > University Coursework" menu, and then select the "Do Homework" interaction.

    • University homework requires the Discover University expansion pack.

Don't have Discover University?

No worries. You can still use this mod with the base game - it'll just ignore the university files.
